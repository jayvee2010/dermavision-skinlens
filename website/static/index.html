Process

How Skin Lens Works

A simple four-step process that combines image quality validation with deep learning to provide responsible risk awareness.

01 ——
📸

Upload Image

Take or upload a clear photo of the skin lesion. The system accepts JPEG and PNG formats and guides you for best results.

02 ——
🔍

Quality Check

Before analysis, Skin Lens validates image quality — checking lighting, focus, and clarity — to ensure accurate results.

03 ——
🧠

AI Analysis

Our AI model examines color, shape, and texture of the lesion to classify it into risk categories.

04 ——
📋

Risk Guidance

Receive a clear risk level with actionable guidance. Not a diagnosis — a responsible nudge toward timely medical care.
